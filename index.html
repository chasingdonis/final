Just a tourist in NYC

Being a tourist in a big city like NYC, the most populated city in United States, everything is new and every area is full of people and energy. New York exerts a significant impact upon commerce, finance, media, art, fashion, research, technology, education, and entertainment. New York is an important center for international diplomacy and has been described as the cultural and financial capital of the world.
